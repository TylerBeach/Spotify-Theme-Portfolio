FireSafe

Created December 2022

What is FireSafe?


FireSafe was a Hackthon project created in a two man team. In short it's a Web Application that functions as a Fire Alert system that notifies users in the vicinity of a fire.


Some Features

  • Users sign up and are stored in the database
  • Users can report a fire in their Area
  • Other users in vicinity of fire are notified
  • Used a machine learning model to locate isolated individuals via satellite imagery that are out of reach of cell service so that first responders can prioritize notifying them as soon as possible
  • Users are also able to see on the map the expected radius where a fire will spread

Froggy Jumper

Created November 2022


What is Froggy Jumper?

Froggy Jumper was my take on the 2013 game Flappy Bird. In Froggy Jumper players hop over the incoming obstacles in a 2D side scroller. Dodge the snakes and eat the flies in the air to raise your score. The game increases in speed the more you progress and slowly becomes night!
